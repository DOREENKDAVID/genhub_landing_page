Services

Geno Hub a user-friendly DNA sequence analysis tool is aimed at simplifying genetic data analysis by offering a variety of features such as sequence visualization and highlight, reverse complement generation for sequences, central dogma features, CG calculation, and sequence length and statistics analysis.

Sequence Visualization and Highlight

Visualize genetic data with easy-to-read displays, making it simpler to interpret sequences

Reverse Complement Generation

Quickly generate the reverse complement of sequences for complementary analysis.

Central Dogma Features

Perform transcription, translation, and reverse transcription operations for genetic sequences.

Genomic Sequence Annotation

Annotate sequences with important genetic information, such as genes, regulatory regions, and functional elements.

CG Calculation,Sequence Length and Statistics Analysis

Analyze sequence length and obtain various statistics to gain insights into your data.

Sequence Alignment

Align multiple sequences to identify similarities, differences, and conserved regions.
